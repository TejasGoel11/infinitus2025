ABOUT INFINITUS

Infinitus is our prestigious IT Fest bringing together tech enthusiasts from across institutions. This single-day hybrid event (both online and offline) celebrates innovation through engaging competitions.

With 6 exciting technical events, Infinitus provides a platform to showcase skills and explore new technologies.
